# Results & Insights — Amazon Marketplace Analytics

## What changed (findings)
- Take rate averaged **~11–13%** with seasonal spikes in Q4 driven by higher AOV categories.
- Refunds remained below **5%**, but concentrated in Electronics (warranty/returns) and Apparel (fit).
- Platinum sellers contributed outsized revenue with lower refund rates, suggesting benefits from VIP enablement.

## What I recommend (decisions)
- Shift paid acquisition budget to categories with high take rate and low refund rate (Home & Kitchen / Beauty).
- Introduce seller education + performance incentives to move Gold sellers toward Platinum behaviors.
- Tighten refund policy for high-risk SKUs and add pre-purchase sizing/spec guidance to reduce returns.
